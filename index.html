Tournament Maths Archive

Developing young mathematicians through collaboration, problem-solving and competition.

Tournament Maths is a Johannesburg mathematics competition that has been running for a number of years, with the aim of developing mathematical thinking in Grade 8, 9 and 10 learners.

Schools send 16 learners to each event. Pairs of learners from one school are partnered with a pair from another school, forming teams of four who work together on an hour-long mathematics paper. The questions draw on curriculum knowledge, creative problem-solving and collaborative thinking.

At the end of each event, a prize-giving is held on the night. Across the seven legs of the year, results are recorded to recognise the top schools and the leading individual participants.
